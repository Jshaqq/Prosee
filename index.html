I Choose You

In a world full of fleeting moments and uncertain paths, I choose you. Again and again. Without doubt. Not just on the days when youre soft and smiling, but even on the days when youre distant, tired, or unsure.

I choose you when the conversations flow like music and when the silence feels heavier than usual. I choose you when youre full of light and even more when you feel like yourre fading. I choose every version of you, the loud laughter, the quiet sadness, the stubborn pride, the always trying your best and the tender love you give in your own way.

Sometimes I feel confused when your warmth feels far away, when your affection quiets down, and I cant quite tell whats on your heart. In those moments, I wonder if youre slipping away or just carrying something silently. But even in my uncertainty, I choose to stay. Because I never stopped choosing you. I never stopped wanting to understand you.

More than anything, I want to be your safe place. Not just a name in your phone or a voice in your ear but a home in your heart. A place where you can fall apart without fear, speak without filtering, feel without shrinking. I want to be where you go when life feels too loud, too cruel, or too heavy. A place where you are always enough.

Loving you isnt a perfect story. Its not always easy, but its real. And real love is what I want. I want the late night talks, the sleepy good mornings, the stubborn disagreements followed by soft apologies. I want your chaos, your calm, your heart even if it doesnt always know how to show love the way I do or the way i want.

Because my heart doesnt want anyone else. It beats a little softer when youre near, aches a little longer when youre far, and always find its way back to you.

So no matter where life pulls us or what we go through, I choose you.
